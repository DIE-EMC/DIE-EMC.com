Destruction Is Evolutionary is an experimental music collective encompassing a variety of musical and philosophical influences with a goal of creating and releasing memorable and thought provoking auditory experiences.


RELEASES


DYING IS EASY

2011

Dying Is Easy, Destruction Is Evolutionary's debut album, is a trip back to the golden days of Wax Trax!, mixing well-placed samples, with heavily layered drum programming and melodic yet brutal synths and guitars. For fans of KMFDM, My Life with the Thrill Kill Kult, and Juno Reactor.

Jeremy Dreher: Synths, Samples, Programming, Vocals
Jason Wright: Guitars

Available at: Bandcamp , iTunes , or Google Play
